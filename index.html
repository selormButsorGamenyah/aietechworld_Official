Our history

AIE TECH WORLD has been around since 2017. The idea began with Selorm Butsor Gamenyah, who realised there had to be a better way to communicate through design, solve world problems through latest technologies, test-driven approach and making good use of the cloud. The goal was to develop high-quality, user-friendly softwares and use creativity with a purpose in a fun driven workspace. These ideas were best implemented in his own company, so he decided to found one. Since then, be it in project meetings, community events, company run, project celebrations: whatever AIE TECH WORLD in involved, we do it with pleasure. And often in the middle of it all; founder Selorm Butsor Gamenyah.
